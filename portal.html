Registration Pathways

Select your preferred registration pathway. Click "Daftar" to proceed to the detailed information page.

Pathway 01
BALLOT

Random ballot system ensuring equal opportunity for all runners. Open to the general public through the My Borobudur Marathon app.

Public Random Draw
Pathway 02
XTRARUN BANK JATENG

Exclusive for Bank Jateng savings account holders meeting minimum balance requirements. Register via branches in Central Java, DI Yogyakarta, and Jakarta.

Bank Jateng Min. Balance
Pathway 03
SAVEDUIT BANK JATENG

Special registration program for Bank Jateng customers. Available at designated branches across Central Java, DI Yogyakarta, and Jakarta.

Customer Exclusive
Pathway 04
URBANA QUALIFIERS

Virtual running qualifier in partnership with Kompas Urbana. Complete missions via the My Borobudur Marathon app to earn your slot.

Virtual Run Kompas
Pathway 05
TRAVEL AGENT PARTNER

Register through official travel partners: Carita Nara, TX Travel Jogja, and Pace n Place. Packages include race entry and travel services.

Carita Nara TX Travel Pace n Place
Pathway 06
INTERNATIONAL RUNNERS

Exclusive registration for international runners through official partners World Marathons and Ahotu. Available for Marathon and Half Marathon categories.

World Marathons Ahotu
Important Notice

All pathways lead to the detailed information page. Registration is only accessible through the My Borobudur Marathon app or authorized partners. Slots are limited and processed on a first-come, first-served or ballot basis depending on the pathway chosen.
